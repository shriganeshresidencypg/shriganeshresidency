FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a co-ed PG? +
No. Shri Ganesh Residency is not a co-ed PG. Male and female residents stay in completely separate flats, ensuring full privacy and a comfortable environment for everyone.
What is the deposit amount? +
The deposit is equal to one month's rent for your chosen room type. It is fully refundable at the time of vacating, provided all rules and notice period conditions are met.
Are electricity and gas bills included in rent? +
No, electricity and gas bills are not included in the rent. They are calculated monthly and divided equally among the roommates sharing the flat. This ensures fair billing for everyone.
How far is the PG from Kharghar Railway Station? +
The PG is approximately 200 metres from Kharghar Railway Station — a 2-minute walk. The Belpada Metro Station (Navi Mumbai Metro Line 1) is also very close, just a 4-minute walk away.
What amenities are provided? +
The PG includes Wi-Fi, washing machine, refrigerator, gas stove, geyser, water purifier, bed and mattress, alternate-day cleaning, CCTV at the main door, and lift access. Basically everything you need to settle in from day one.
Can I stay for just a few days? +
Yes! Short-term accommodation is available at ₹500 per day, subject to availability. Please contact us via WhatsApp or call before arriving to confirm availability and terms.
Is the PG suitable for students? +
Absolutely. The PG is ideally located near NIFT, CDAC, ITM Business School, and other colleges in Kharghar. The affordable pricing, reliable Wi-Fi, and proximity to public transport make it a top choice for students.
How do I book a room? +
The easiest way is to send us a WhatsApp message on 7080503099 with your preferred room type and move-in date. You can also call or email us. We'll confirm availability and guide you through the simple joining process.
